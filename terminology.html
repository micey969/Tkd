Lastly, we have the core beliefs of Taekwondo.

Student Pledge

As a student of Taekwondo, I do solemnly pledge to abide by the principles of the St. Vincent & the Grenadines Taekwondo Association. To strive always to be modest, courteous and respectful to all members, and in particular to my seniors. To put the art into use only for self-defence or in defence of the weak and never to abuse my knowledge of the art.

Tenets

  • Courtesy (Ye Ui)
  • Integrity (Yom Chi)
  • Perseverance (In Nae)
  • Self-Control (Guk Gi)
  • Indomitable Spirit (Bek Jul Bul Gul)

Oaths

  • I will observe the tenets of Taekwondo
  • I will never misuse Taekwondo
  • I will respect my seniors and my instructors
  • I will strive to build a more peaceful world
  • I will be a champion for freedom and justice

Commandments

  • Respect your parents
  • Respect your brothers and sisters
  • Respect your elders
  • Respect your teachers
  • Loyalty to your friends
  • Finish what you start
